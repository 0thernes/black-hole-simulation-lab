## Black-hole shadows / Kerr spin / EHT  *(directly validates the renderer)*

- **Photon regions, shadow observables and constraints from M87* of a
  Kerr–Newman-like black hole in Bumblebee gravity surrounded by plasma** —
  J. Zhang et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. Separable null geodesics in a
  power-law plasma around a charged, Lorentz-violating rotating black hole; spin
  and the Bumblebee parameter mainly distort the shadow while charge and plasma
  shrink it radially, and the M87* angular diameter (42±3 µas) plus circularity
  and axis-ratio bounds still admit a slice of the parameter space. *tier:
  observational_constraint.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/eda1682ef2ef50f391da0fc5e0217eb6/
- **Photon Ring Astrometry I: A Simple Spin Measurement Technique for
  High-Resolution Images of M87*** — D. E. Gates et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*.
  Proposes the displacement between the n=1 lensed sub-image and the direct image
  (split parallel/transverse to the jet-aligned spin axis) as a spin observable;
  GRMHD magnetically-arrested-disk models show ≲0.1 µas relative astrometry
  constrains spin to ~9% (prograde) / ~22% (retrograde) without geometric
  emission modeling. *tier: numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/60f887f8ec955db5ba1c6abd57c3406b/
- **Ring Asymmetry and Spin in M87*** — V. Bernshteyn et al., 2026, *The
  Astrophysical Journal*. Compares the observed brightness-asymmetry distribution
  of the EHT ring against GRMHD predictions across spin; three EHT epochs of
  strongly-magnetized (MAD) models marginally disfavor |a*|≲0.2, consistent with
  the Blandford–Znajek expectation of nonzero M87* spin. *tier:
  observational_constraint.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/6ac7e90bada6535eac7ed8c4ac728e9f/

## Numerical relativity / ringdown / QNM  *(adjacent observational GR)*

- **GWTC-4.0: Tests of General Relativity. III. Tests of the Remnants** —
  LIGO–Virgo–KAGRA Collaboration, 2026, *arXiv preprint*. First TGR remnant suite
  on 42 events from O4a plus prior runs: three ringdown consistency tests against
  the Kerr QNM spectrum and four echo searches; remnants stay consistent with GR
  (GR at the boundary of the ~98.6% credible region, dropping toward ~92% once the
  loud GW250114 is included), and no post-merger echoes are found. *tier:
  observational_constraint.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/3b053a9363f55f068d69f136b2e66263/
- **Highly eccentric non-spinning binary black hole mergers: quadrupolar
  post-merger waveforms** — N. Rao et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. Builds
  closed-form (2,2) post-merger waveforms from 233 eccentric RIT-catalog NR runs
  via Bayesian time-dependent QNM amplitudes as polynomials in mass ratio and the
  effective energy/angular momentum at merger; ~10⁻³ mismatches (validated on SXS)
  even at near-extreme eccentricity, pluggable into EOB/phenomenological IMR
  models. *tier: numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/259831acd2f95f7285685a75bf5a90df/
- **Lessons from binary dynamics of inspiralling equal-mass boson-star mergers** —
  T. Evstafyeva et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. NR simulations of equal-mass
  boson-star binaries matched to post-Newtonian inspiral and to remnant QNM
  extraction; deviations from BBH peak in the late inspiral/merger, certain
  scalar phase offsets excite subdominant odd-m multipoles absent for BBH, and
  IMR-consistency tests are shown to break the waveform degeneracy with current
  approximants. *tier: numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/96ebe59128a4501aba940e380a69c2b0/

## Quantum gravity / information paradox / holography  *(speculative context — label, do not let it touch the kernel)*

- **Thermodynamics and information recovery of Schwarzschild-AdS black holes in
  conformal Killing gravity** — Y. Ladghami et al., 2026, *Eur. Phys. J. C*.
  Treats Λ as pressure and the conformal-Killing-gravity parameter as an
  independent variable; the area law survives but the parameter's sign controls
  Van-der-Waals-like criticality, and the island prescription restores the Page
  curve (late-time entropy saturating at twice Bekenstein–Hawking) with Page time
  expressed in thermodynamic quantities. *tier: speculative_extension.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/6618ee23e1ef55a69dee39122e70ae10/
- **de Sitter extremal surfaces, time contours, complexifications and
  pseudo-entropies** — K. Narayan, 2026, *arXiv preprint*. No-boundary dS extremal
  surfaces for future-boundary subregions: large subregions admit
  timelike+Euclidean and complex surfaces (small ones only complex), with area
  integrals defined over complex-time contours; multiple deformable contours yield
  equal areas, hinting that complex dS replica geometries encode AdS entropy
  inequalities by analytic continuation. *tier: speculative_extension.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/c05798582e455d519e240381c5edfce4/
- **Thin-shell wormhole from modified Bardeen-AdS black hole** — B. Sarkar et al.,
  2026, *Eur. Phys. J. C*. Cut-and-paste construction of a thin-shell wormhole
  from two modified Bardeen-AdS copies; Israel-formalism surface stress tensor,
  explicit energy-condition violation, and linearised stability under barotropic,
  phantom-like, and generalized-Chaplygin equations of state as functions of throat
  radius, charge, and Λ. *tier: speculative_extension.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/d3fd03c2a8995649a838dd26288e5f5d/
