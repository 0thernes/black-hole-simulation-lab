## Black-hole shadows / Kerr spin / EHT  *(directly validates the renderer)*

- **Constraining Nonsingular Black Holes with a Minkowski Core via EHT
  Observations of M87* and Sgr A*** — M.-X. Li et al., 2026, *Universe*.
  Systematically bounds the quantum-gravity parameter α and regularization index
  n for three Minkowski-core nonsingular black-hole families against the observed
  M87*/Sgr A* shadow sizes; M87* yields the tighter constraints, and increasing α
  contracts and dims the shadow while larger n enlarges it and washes out the
  inter-type differences. *tier: observational_constraint.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/db3dfc888ce7562daad1d31b45612b8e/
- **Plasma modified black hole shadows and weak gravitational lensing of
  Einstein–Maxwell-dilaton spacetimes: constraints from the EHT** — K. Ghaderi,
  2026, *Classical and Quantum Gravity*. Einstein–Maxwell-dilaton black holes in
  a cold, dispersive plasma: the shadow radius shrinks monotonically with plasma
  strength despite an outward photon-sphere shift, charge suppresses the shadow
  while dilaton coupling enlarges it, and EHT ring-size + asymmetry bounds for
  M87*/Sgr A* carve out the compatible (charge, coupling, plasma) parameter
  space. *tier: observational_constraint.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/cd2a428e352a500c865ae0824be97cbd/

## Numerical relativity / ringdown / QNM / GW modeling  *(adjacent observational GR)*

- **Modeling the merger-ringdown of an eccentric test-mass inspiral into a Kerr
  black hole using the effective-one-body framework** — G. Faggioli et al., 2026,
  *arXiv preprint*. Time-domain Teukolsky waveforms sourced by EOB trajectories
  for equatorial-eccentric plunges into Kerr (spin a∈[−0.9,0.9], e_LSO up to 0.9,
  all standard SEOBNR harmonics); eccentricity mainly reshapes the final
  pre-merger peak while the ringdown stays largely insensitive, feeding a
  forthcoming SEOB-TMLE eccentric inspiral-merger-ringdown model that also
  accounts for QNM mode mixing. *tier: numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/22a03290d8a755a08c216c59b92dd6f2/
- **Branch-dependent ringdown in black-bounce spacetimes: imprints of
  matter-source ambiguity on quasinormal modes** — H. Yang et al., 2026, *arXiv
  preprint*. In the Simpson–Visser black-bounce (a regular black hole for a≤2M
  that becomes a traversable wormhole for a>2M), axial-perturbation QNMs extracted
  by Prony fitting differ between an anisotropic-fluid and a nonlinear-
  electrodynamics interpretation of the same metric — faster damping in the
  black-hole branch, longer-lived fundamental modes in the wormhole branch —
  arguing GW spectroscopy can break the matter-source degeneracy of exotic compact
  objects. *tier: numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/320f29902b345483b9a549c14035e20f/
- **A Semi-analytic Approach to Ringdown Gravitational Waves** — M. Suzuki et al.,
  2026, *Journal of Physics: Conference Series*. A computationally efficient
  Bayesian ringdown framework that Gram–Schmidt-orthonormalizes the quasinormal-
  mode basis to reduce inter-mode parameter correlations and analytically
  marginalize the likelihood over mode amplitudes, demonstrated on a
  damped-sinusoid signal as a step toward robust multi-QNM tests of GR. *tier:
  numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/4c267e6a28f552c099f79a5660988442/

## Quantum gravity / information paradox / holography  *(speculative context — label, do not let it touch the kernel)*

- **Asymptotic Algebras and Holography of Information in the CGHS Model** —
  W. A. Dar et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. Constructs the radiative phase space
  and future-null-infinity asymptotic algebras of the 2D CGHS dilaton-gravity
  model coupled to a massless scalar, establishing Holography of Information
  (right-moving state recoverable from an arbitrarily small neighbourhood of null
  infinity) and arguing that if left/right Hilbert-space factorization holds, the
  island entropy cannot equal any one-sided asymptotic von Neumann entropy — a
  CGHS factorization problem distinguishing the HoI and island Page curves.
  *tier: speculative_extension.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/eb03e698801d5195be1e17d03bdc67e2/
- **Timelike Entanglement Entropy of Hawking Radiation** — Y. Ladghami et al.,
  2026, *arXiv preprint*. Analytically continues black-hole spacetimes to define a
  "timelike" entanglement entropy exhibiting a sequence of timelike Page times at
  which it equals the Bekenstein–Hawking entropy; applied to Schwarzschild,
  Reissner–Nordström, higher-dimensional/braneworld, Kerr and Myers–Perry holes,
  the recurrence times track surface gravity, charge, spin and dimension while
  encoding information entirely in the radiation, without islands or firewalls.
  *tier: speculative_extension.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/c68258594c2e5f808685440c76150f45/
- **Escape of quantum information across an analogue black hole horizon** —
  Z. Liu et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. Credible-but-fringe: realizes an analogue
  black-hole horizon via position-dependent coupling in an XY spin chain, derives
  Page-curve-like radiation-entropy behavior, and tracks entanglement and
  coherence transferring from an interior subsystem across the effective horizon —
  a quantum-simulation perspective on how information may leave a black hole.
  *tier: speculative_extension.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/1a56901f4971574591799966e545c5ac/
