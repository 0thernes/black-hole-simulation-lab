## Black-hole shadows / Kerr spin / EHT  *(directly validates the renderer)*

- **Black Hole Polarimetry III: Universal Polarization of Synchrotron Radiation
  at the Horizon** — A. Chael et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. For synchrotron
  emission from the base of horizon-threading field lines in a time-stationary,
  axisymmetric, degenerate Kerr magnetosphere, the observed linear-polarization
  pattern is shown to be *universal* — fixed entirely by spin and observer
  inclination and independent of magnetic-field geometry — with a closed-form
  analytic formula that time-averaged GRMHD images approach, so that microarcsecond
  VLBI of M87* could measure spin and confirm Blandford–Znajek spin-energy
  extraction. *tier: numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/0bd7431e76675aebad01f0eac73d3ca5/
- **On the observational distinguishability of the Kerr and Kerr–Hayward metrics
  to EHT** — N. Bukowiecka et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. New GRMHD simulations
  of magnetized plasma in a singularity-free Kerr–Hayward spacetime, pushed through
  an extended EHT polarized-radiative-transfer pipeline, reproduce the Kerr
  fluid quantities (magnetic-flux parameter, jet efficiency) and image observables
  (polarization pattern, photon-ring structure) so closely that the regular
  correction is effectively indistinguishable from Kerr in current EHT
  measurements — a cautionary result for horizon-scale tests of the metric.
  *tier: numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/4744a935c49b595d8a21f3c26d1aa935/
- **Polarized radiative transfer of Kerr–Newman black hole** — X. Li et al., 2026,
  *European Physical Journal C*. Replaces the symmetry-restricted Walker–Penrose
  method with an ODE framework that co-evolves the photon orbit and the
  polarization parallel-transport equations in arbitrary backgrounds, then applies
  it to prograde/retrograde disks around a charged Kerr–Newman black hole: growing
  charge compresses and distorts the electric-vector position-angle structure on
  photon-ring scales and induces localized rotations and asymmetries, offering a
  potential diagnostic of nonzero black-hole charge. *tier:
  numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/77d346bd19dc545cbd061b00d1c34370/

## Numerical relativity / ringdown / QNM / GW modeling  *(adjacent observational GR)*

- **Black Hole Ringdown Seen in Photon Polarization Swings** — J. Huang et al.,
  2026, *arXiv preprint*. Develops a covariant perturbative framework for polarized
  photon propagation through a perturbed spacetime and derives a compact expression
  for the polarization-angle swing during Kerr ringdown, showing it is time-locked
  to the quasinormal modes; dynamical ray-tracing confirms that photons grazing the
  strong-field region undergo an achromatic, damped ~10° PA oscillation tracking the
  ringdown — opening a polarimetric, EHT-scale window onto black-hole mergers
  distinct from the gravitational-wave channel. *tier: numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/35da747235a75d64b9c3cb4c4de22a61/
- **Ringdown Signatures of Dehnen Dark Matter Halos: Fluid Modes and Detectability
  with Space-Based Detectors** — M. Liang et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. Building
  on Cardoso et al.'s fully relativistic framework (dark matter minimally coupled to
  gravity), constructs numerical ringdown waveforms for Dehnen-profile halos around
  supermassive black holes, converts them into 2nd-generation TDI data streams, and
  runs Bayesian parameter estimation: dark matter imprints sizable late-time *fluid
  modes*, with spikier profiles leaving stronger signatures and improving joint
  inference of black-hole and halo parameters for LISA/Taiji/TianQin. *tier:
  numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/e6c4218c91225e98a594838fc522923b/
- **Hankel low-rank matrix approximation for gravitational-wave data analysis** —
  N. Geissler et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. Recasts extraction of superposed
  damped sinusoids (the ringdown QNM problem) as structured low-rank approximation
  of a Hankel-embedded time series — a sum of n damped modes gives a rank-2n matrix —
  and benchmarks ESPRIT, Cadzow iterations, and IRLS on synthetic and numerical-
  relativity waveforms, reaching near-Fisher-bound (inverse-square-in-SNR) mismatch
  scaling and validating QNM-frequency recovery from ringdown as a fast preprocessing
  route for overlapping next-generation (LISA) signals. *tier:
  numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/9b456daa470258a19c44f2b844c2fcfd/

## Quantum gravity / information paradox / holography  *(speculative context — label, do not let it touch the kernel)*

- **Probing the Factorized Island Branch with the Capacity of Entanglement in JT
  Gravity** — R. Arias et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. In JT gravity coupled to a
  large-c bath, shows that the *capacity of entanglement* detects replica structure
  that survives beyond the von Neumann entropy: in the late-time, high-temperature
  factorized island branch the entropy plateau is rigid at first nontrivial order
  while the capacity acquires a definite correction — a sharp semiclassical example
  that finite-n replica data around an island saddle carry physical, observable
  information about how the saddle is assembled. *tier: speculative_extension.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/d8c2176938e45612aa2d7d3b083e0b3d/
- **Entanglement islands, fuzzballs and stretched horizons** — D. Ageev et al.,
  2026, *arXiv preprint*. Implements the island prescription in fuzzball-inspired
  models by replacing the horizon with a reflecting stretched-horizon boundary: the
  boundary modifies the island saddle and, over a parameter range, produces the
  "blinking island" effect that reinstates an information-paradox analogue; extending
  to higher dimensions and to stringy superstrata/bubbling geometries, the existence
  of island solutions is found to hinge sensitively on boundary conditions and the
  near-cap behavior of the geometric area, and is not guaranteed in general. *tier:
  speculative_extension.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/a7a2b23b751653ae974d95c28ac576cf/
