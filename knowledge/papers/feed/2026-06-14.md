## Black-hole shadows / Kerr spin / EHT  *(directly validates the renderer)*

- **Shadow of a Nonlinear Electromagnetic Generalized Kerr–Newman–AdS Black Hole** —
  M. Fathi, 2025, *Galaxies*. Celestial-coordinate shadow at finite observer
  distance; spin controls deformation, nonlinear electrodynamics modifies the
  photon region; confronted with EHT M87*/Sgr A*. *tier: numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/c232c220945d557ab63603c7f3aa8d4a/
- **Deciphering signatures of Kerr-Sen black holes in presence of plasma from the
  EHT data** — S. Sahoo et al., 2025, *JCAP*. Dilaton charge + plasma; M87*
  favors Kerr (rules out r₂>0.48), Sgr A* marginal Kerr-Sen. *tier:
  observational_constraint.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/a508e04fa71e559a8873794acb7ff87b/
- **Plasma effects on gravitational lensing and shadow observables of a Kerr-like
  black hole in a dark matter halo** — C. McMillin et al., 2026. Null geodesics
  in homogeneous/inhomogeneous plasma; spin enlarges shadow + deformation;
  inclination reduces both. *tier: numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/996b9751842357698244c730f4f6f95c/
