## Black-hole shadows / Kerr spin / EHT  *(directly validates the renderer)*

- **Observational Signatures of Rotating Ayón-Beato-García Black Holes: Shadows,
  Accretion Disks and Images** — Z. Ban et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. Shadows,
  thin-disk images and redshift maps of a rotating Ayón-Beato-García (regular,
  NED-sourced) black hole with mass M, spin a and electric charge ζ: shadow size
  shrinks with ζ and adopts a distinctive "D"-shaped morphology near extremality,
  while higher inclination separates direct and lensed images into a hat-like
  structure. Confronting the shadow diameter with EHT M87*/Sgr A* at 17°/50°/90°
  yields the joint bound 0.133 M < ζ < 0.214 M. *tier: observational_constraint.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/a36a91587fca5772a5267c0ba0198161/
- **Reshaping the inner shadow of a Kerr black hole by a torn accretion disk** —
  S. Hu et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. Relativistic backward ray-tracing of a
  phenomenological torn-disk model (a tilted disk broken into sub-disks by
  frame-dragging) around a Kerr black hole: the discontinuity between sub-disks
  and the outer tilt angle severely erode the inner shadow, producing bifurcated
  shadows, crescent-like structures and multiple orders of shadow rings that are
  hard to reproduce with equatorial accretion — implying inner-shadow-only tests
  of gravity are fundamentally insufficient. *tier: numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/80385e5579df5966a237b1d10343fd91/
- **Shadow of Bonanno–Reuter Black Hole in Plasma Medium: Insights from EHT Sgr A*
  Observations** — S. Kala, 2026, *Nuclear Physics B*. Shadow of the
  renormalization-group-improved Bonanno-Reuter spacetime (quantum-gravity
  correction via a scale-dependent parameter ω̃) in a non-uniform pressureless
  plasma: the angular shadow radius deviates systematically from Schwarzschild,
  and EHT Sgr A* data constrain ω̃ — but plasma and quantum-gravity effects are
  observationally degenerate, a degeneracy next-generation EHT should break.
  *tier: observational_constraint.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/a935b0f89600562e8a34e5394596c32b/

## Numerical relativity / ringdown / QNM / GW modeling  *(adjacent observational GR)*

- **Plunge-merger-ringdown tests of general relativity with GW250114** —
  L. Grimaldi et al., 2026, *Physical Review D*. Using a parametrized
  effective-one-body waveform for spin-precessing binaries, deviations from GR are
  constrained across the plunge-merger-ringdown of GW250114 (the clearest event to
  date): the (2,2) peak amplitude and instantaneous frequency are bounded to ~10%
  and ~4% at 90% credibility — two-to-four times tighter than GW150914 — with the
  (4,4) merger frequency constrained for the first time (~6%). The most precise
  tests of GR in the nonlinear regime yet. *tier: observational_constraint.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/128ed9f913ef5a03b4722ddf200e5ebb/
- **A self-consistent EOB–Teukolsky framework for generic extreme mass-ratio
  inspirals** — X. Zhong et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. A full-relativistic EMRI
  waveform model that self-consistently feeds analytic, mass-ratio-informed
  geodesics in a deformed Kerr metric into the Teukolsky source term, coupled to a
  high-performance homogeneous-Teukolsky solver for the tens of thousands of modes
  an accurate EMRI needs; finite-mass-ratio corrections beyond the test-particle
  limit produce measurable changes to fluxes and accumulated phase for LISA data
  analysis. *tier: numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/0bca3802ad5d531eb537a3fa6a1051b8/
- **Imprints of quantum gravity effects on gravitational waves: a comparative
  study using extreme mass-ratio inspirals** — R.-T. Chen et al., 2026, *arXiv
  preprint*. Two covariant-Hamiltonian loop-quantum-gravity black-hole models
  (quantum correction ζ) are probed with EMRIs: improved augmented-analytic-kludge
  waveforms in each LQG background are compared to Schwarzschild via faithfulness
  analysis, quantifying LISA's detectability of the deformation and deriving
  detection-threshold constraints on ζ — the first model imprints far more
  strongly than the second. *tier: numerical_approximation.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/7a82fd42b9a256acba34e3d41a0e8c8f/

## Quantum gravity / information paradox / holography  *(speculative context — label, do not let it touch the kernel)*

- **A Reverse Black Hole Information Problem** — J. de Boer et al., 2026, *arXiv
  preprint*. Formation, detection and coarse-graining of black holes in AdS/CFT:
  CFT states dual to black-hole formation/evaporation are built by colliding bulk
  wavepackets at trans-Planckian energy, boundary probes are proposed that
  distinguish small AdS black holes within the microcanonical ensemble, and
  several coarse-graining prescriptions (averaging over CFT data, Hamiltonians,
  time windows) are compared — clarifying how semiclassical mixed-state Hawking
  radiation emerges from ensemble-averaged exact unitary dynamics. *tier:
  speculative_extension.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/faa726f0fa14581eb7d00dedcd63bcab/
- **The Fate of Nucleated Black Holes in de Sitter Quantum Gravity** — X. Shi
  et al., 2026, *arXiv preprint*. Revisiting the Euclidean Nariai instanton for
  maximal-mass black-hole nucleation in de Sitter: once an observer is included,
  the gravitational path integral supplies the imaginary phase needed for a
  transition rate. The previously claimed "anti-evaporation" channel is argued to
  be unphysical (a singular-horizon state); in a smooth state the nucleated hole
  evaporates normally back to empty de Sitter, making the whole process a
  Boltzmann fluctuation. *tier: speculative_extension.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/56fc2255172d5ea7a7be164825a62dc5/
- **Holographic timelike entanglement and subregion complexity with scalar hair**
  — H. L. Prihadi et al., 2026, *Journal of High Energy Physics*. Timelike
  entanglement entropy (HTEE) and timelike subregion complexity of a thermal CFT
  deformed by a relevant scalar operator, dual to a hairy AdS black hole: the
  scalar deformation breaks the Δt-invariance of the imaginary part of HTEE seen
  in pure AdS3/BTZ, analytic continuation of the CFT temporal entanglement fails
  to reproduce HTEE under deformation, while the complexity=volume subregion
  complexity stays real-valued as a complementary interior probe. *tier:
  speculative_extension.*
  https://consensus.app/papers/details/48e26ff2b37b59f6b8cc069c00a0dd16/
